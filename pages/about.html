Born Into the Garden

Green Seasons was founded by Tim Harris, a second-generation horticulturalist who grew up with a trowel in hand and a genuine love of plants. That heritage runs through everything we do, from the way we approach a new planting scheme to how we advise clients on the best time to prune a struggling shrub.

Unlike many contractors who simply cut and move on, we are true plant people. We understand how plants grow, when they thrive, and what they need to reach their full potential. That depth of knowledge means you receive guidance and care that goes far beyond a standard maintenance visit.

Based in we serve private homeowners, commercial landlords, housing developments, and local authority spaces right across Oxfordshire, including Oxford, Abingdon, Didcot, Wantage, Witney and the surrounding villages.
